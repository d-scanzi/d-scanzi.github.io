10000 Neons

Inspired by the show 10000 Hours by the Gravity & Other Myths group. Specifically, the seven-segments display clock they have in the background. This is the result. Not a clock, but a series of neon lights arranged in simple shapes, squares or curves, which repeats (or not) to create grids of light. A "wobble" is added (or not) to create a sense of instability and dynamicity. Code and more pieces available on Github.
